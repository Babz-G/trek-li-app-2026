Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Thank you for using the Trek Long Island 2026 official convention app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information the app does and does not collect.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The app does not require you to create an account or provide any personal details such as your name, email address, or location.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores two small pieces of information locally on your device only:

  • Your bookmarked schedule events (saved to My Schedule)
  • Your light or dark mode preference

This data is stored using your device's local storage and never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, and we have no access to it.

Third-Party Services

The app contains links to external websites including the Trek Long Island ticketing page, social media profiles, and sponsor websites. Once you leave the app and visit these sites, their own privacy policies apply. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of any third-party sites.

Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any analytics tools, tracking software, or advertising networks. We do not track your behavior within the app in any way.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no personal data from any users, no special handling is required.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. We encourage you to review it periodically.
